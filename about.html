Company Profile

We are the fastest emerging business group in the horizon of business sector of Bangladesh. We have started our journey with "Dayim Landmark Limited" to provide clean and green habitation to our customers, and then extend our wings in tourism sector through "Dayim Tours & Logistics" by offering exclusive package tours and student counseling, in agro industry through "Dayim Agro & Trading" and in engineering sector through "Dayim Power & Engineering". We have an energetic, enthusiastic and passionate team containing diversified professionals who keep its pace in the right direction. We work with the motto "We strive to make your dream true".

  • Vision

  • We work with a vision of providing quality service to our customers by maintaining trust, cooperation and amiable relationship.

  • Mission

  • We work with a mission of "Making your dream true". Our goal is to bring new dimension in the concept of business field and ensure green environment by promoting, patronizing and facilitating. We strive for a developed Bangladesh by creating employment opportunity, eradicating poverty and promoting clean environment.
