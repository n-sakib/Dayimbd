Bondhon Residential Project

Dhaka city is been expanding through past few years, it expanded in north up to Tongi, Gazipur; in east up to Kachpur, Narayanganj; in south up to Dhaka-Maowa, Rajuk-Jhilmil. Now there is no place to expand further in these three phases except to expand in west. And the expansion in west becomes obvious because of the proposed 170 ft Western Bypass. This new expansion is needed to be developed in such a manner so that the unplanned and environment pollution of the central city will not influence this part. We, Dayim Landmark Limited are working with a mission to develop this newer western part in a well planned and well facilitate fashion through our project "Bondhon".

Why "Bondhon" is different than other residential project?

  • This is the only project of which the Whole Land of the project is purchased before the selling started.
  • It is situated just beside the DAP proposed 170 feet Western Bypass Road (Adjoined to Dhaka-Mawa and Dhaka-Aricha Road).
  • Distance from Farmgate to the project is half of the distance of Motijheel-to-Mirpur.
  • There are several link roads attached with the project and currently it has Gas and Electricity connection.
  • In this project there will be 80 feet avenue road and minimum 25 feet internal roads.
  • Completely noise free and refreshing environment.
  • Some renowned organizations including Hospitals, Schools etc. have already signed agreement of taking land in this project.
  • Taking account of the middle class people we designed a convenient 6 years monthly installment payment schedule for paying the total amount of 8,50,000 taka (per Flat-1020 sft/per 1 Katha land).
